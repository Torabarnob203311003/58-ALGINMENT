About 58 Truck &
Trailer Alignment

Bakersfield 58 Truck & Trailer Alignment provides truck alignment services promptly and professionally. We eliminate the hassle of getting your semi truck, trailer, RV, bus or coach aligned, making your maintenance experience easy and stress-free.

We employ highly-trained truck alignment technicians and utilize state-of-the-art Hunter equipment for laser-precision diagnosis. We can find out quickly where the angles are off and adjust them accordingly. We can usually get your vehicle aligned the day you bring it in.

Call us today if you suspect your vehicle is out alignment causing uneven rapid tire wear, noisy steering, pulling to right or left or squealing tires.
